iShop Policy

At iShop, we are committed to providing our customers with the best possible experience when they visit our ice cream shop. As an Indian ice cream e-commerce company, we have gathered sellers from all around the world to provide our customers with a diverse range of products.
    Customer Service
  1. We strive to provide our customers with friendly, courteous, and efficient service.
  2. We will do our best to accommodate any special requests or dietary needs that our customers may have.
  3. If you are not satisfied with your purchase, please let us know and we will do our best to make it right.
    Product Quality
  1. We use only the freshest and highest quality ingredients in our ice cream and other products.
  2. Our ice cream is made on-site in small batches to ensure the best possible taste and texture.
  3. We regularly test our products to ensure that they meet our high standards for quality and safety.
    Pricing and Payment
  1. Our prices are competitive and reflect the high quality of our products.
  2. We accept cash, credit cards, and other forms of payment.
  3. We reserve the right to change our prices at any time, but will always notify our customers in advance of any price changes.
    Delivery and Pickup
  1. We offer both delivery and pickup options for our products.
  2. Our delivery service is available within a limited geographic area and may be subject to additional fees.
  3. We ask that customers provide accurate and complete information when placing orders for delivery or pickup.
    Privacy and Security
  1. We respect our customers' privacy and will never share their personal information with third parties without their consent.
  2. We use secure payment processing systems to protect our customers' financial information.
  3. We take all necessary precautions to ensure the security of our customers' data, including using encryption and other security measures.
    Employee Conduct
  1. Our employees are expected to conduct themselves in a professional and respectful manner at all times.
  2. We provide ongoing training to our employees to ensure that they are knowledgeable about our products and policies.
  3. Any employee who violates our policies or engages in any form of misconduct will be subject to disciplinary action, up to and including termination.
    Health and Safety
  1. We take the health and safety of our customers and employees very seriously.
  2. We follow strict hygiene and sanitation protocols to ensure that our products are safe for consumption.
  3. We regularly inspect our facilities and equipment to ensure that they meet our high standards for cleanliness and safety.
Conclusion

At iShop, we are committed to providing our customers with the best possible experience. We hope that our policies help to ensure that our customers always receive high-quality products, excellent service, and a safe and enjoyable environment. If you have any questions or concerns about our policies, please do not hesitate to contact us. Thank you for choosing iShop!
